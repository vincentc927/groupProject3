Mental Wellness
for Singles

Being single can often weigh on your mental health. Single Breath is designed to lift you to an improved state of mind and encourage you to keep your pursuits alive.

Live Out Loud

Single Breath gives you the incentive to put your mental wellness front and center with the Mood Booster. You also have access to the Progress Tracker and recommended Resources, which are great tools for aiming for your best.

"I was so depressed and hopeless about being single that it affected my mental health and I could barely function. When I tried Single Breath, I was able to take baby steps out of my funk and get my life back."

Alisha Roberts, Age 36
